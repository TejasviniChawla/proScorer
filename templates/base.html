KEY FEATURES OF ProScorer

WHAT?

Smartly generates fill in the blank questions from any given text from a chapter. Making it the best way to get an intricate and thorough understanding of the chapter and make sure students never lose a mark

WHERE?

This would be a great help in revison for tests, especially for boards 1. Helps students scale greater heights by giving them the most perfect, most detailed and most accurate rivison they could ask for.

HOW?

When the user inputs the paragraph, the program selects 5 random lines from it, after that, it selects an important word from each line and then relaces the word with a blank, giving us a fill up question.

Languages

nltk package in python was used to enable it to do the processing and the website was implemented through flask and html.
